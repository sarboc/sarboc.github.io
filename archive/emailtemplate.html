Get Ready to Gaelebrate

   

JOIN US on Saturday, Oct. 6, from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. 

 

The entire Saint Mary's family is invited. That includes alumni, friends and, actually, everyone in the Bay Area. We'll showcase what makes Saint Mary's special --

with interactive displays, highlights of Saint Mary's current programs, an exploration of our 150 years of history, live music and drama, rides, athletic and academic demonstrations, gourmet food trucks and more. 

 

It's a GAELEBRATION! And it's free and open to the public. 

 

Getting to Gaelebration

 

From Lafayette and Beyond

If you live outside the Moraga area, the best way to avoid traffic on roads leading to Moraga is to take a shuttle from Lafayette BART.

 

BART Shuttle Parking and Pickup will be available at:

  • BART station entrance
  • The northeast lot, just north of Oak Hill Road on Deer Hill Road.

Shuttles will run approximately every 10 minutes from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.

 

From the Moraga Area

If you plan to drive, please carpool to one of the satellite lots and take a shuttle to campus. Only presenters, performers, vendors and people with a valid California disabled placard will be guaranteed a parking spot on campus after 10 a.m. on Saturday. Once the parking lots are full, all other drivers will be redirected to off-site parking, so it will be much easier and faster to park off-site and take a shuttle.

 

Off-Site Parking and Shuttles

There will be free parking and shuttle pickup at the following locations in Moraga from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m. on Saturday:

  • Near 5A Rent-A-Space, 455 Moraga Road
  • SMC Rheem Campus, 380 Moraga Road
  • Unpaved lot across from Moraga Commons, at the corner of Saint Mary's Road & Moraga Road

Shuttle Routes

  • 5A Rent-a-Space lot - Rheem Center - Campus (12-15 minute loop)
  • Ascot Drive/Place - Moraga Road/Donald Drive - unpaved lot across from Moraga Commons - Campus - (12-15 minute loop)
  • Lafayette BART gate - Lafayette BART parking - Campus (10-12 minute loop)
Please be respectful of the citizens of Moraga; do not park on any private property or in mall parking lots.
 

Bicycle Parking

Bicycle self-parking will be available on the grassy area between the guard station and the baseball field.
